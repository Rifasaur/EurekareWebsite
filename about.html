Eurekare Corporation

WHO WE ARE

Innovative E-care, simplified for you.

EUREKARE CORP. is a Healthcare Information System Service Provider based in the Philippines with investors from Taiwan and the Philippines. We bring to the table the eCARE EMR Yakap/Konsulta Project, which provides an Electronic Medical Record (EMR) System integrated with the Yakap/Konsulta Module in accordance with the requirements of the Universal Healthcare Act — offered as an UNSOLICITED PROPOSAL with minimum initial investment from the government.

Our mission is to modernize healthcare delivery by bridging the gap between patients, providers, and government health programs through innovative, accessible, and secure digital health solutions.
