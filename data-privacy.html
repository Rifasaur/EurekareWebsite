Eurekare Corporation Data Privacy Policy

Data Privacy Statement

The eCARE EMR and PhilHealth YAKAP Registration System, developed by Eurekare Corp., is committed to protecting the privacy, confidentiality, and security of all personal and health information collected, processed, and stored through its platform. This commitment aligns with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173), the PhilHealth Data Sharing Guidelines, and applicable Department of Health (DOH) administrative orders.

Scope of Data Collection

The system collects and processes the following categories of information solely for legitimate healthcare and governance purposes:

  • Personal Identification Data Name, date of birth, address, contact details, and demographic information.
  • Health Information Medical history, diagnoses, laboratory results, prescriptions, and treatment records.
  • Registration Data YAKAP enrollment details, PhilHealth membership information, and eCARE ID issuance records.
  • System Access Logs User credentials, timestamps, and audit trails for authorized access monitoring.

Purpose of Data Processing

All data collected are used to fulfill specific strategic operations and administrative workflows:

  • Facilitate citizen registration and health service access under the YAKAP Program.
  • Enable PhilHealth eClaims and eKonsulta transactions.
  • Support LGU health governance and SDG Goal 3 monitoring.
  • Ensure continuity of care across accredited facilities.
  • Maintain audit-ready documentation for compliance and accountability.

Data Protection Measures

The eCARE EMR System employs strict organizational, physical, and technical safeguards to ensure absolute data integrity and confidentiality:

  • Role-based access controls and user authentication.
  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest.
  • Secure servers with continuous monitoring and backup routines.
  • Regular privacy impact assessments and internal compliance audits.
  • Continuous staff training on data privacy handling and legal confidentiality obligations.

Data Sharing and Retention

  • Data are shared only with authorized healthcare providers, PhilHealth, and LGU offices for legitimate purposes.
  • No information is disclosed to external third parties without explicit consent or explicit legal authorization.
  • Records are retained securely in strict accordance with DOH and PhilHealth retention schedules, after which secure disposal procedures are applied.

Rights of Data Subjects

Under the provisions of the Data Privacy Act, all registered individuals retain the complete entitlement to:

  • Access their personal and medical records history seamlessly.
  • Correct, modify, or update inaccurate or outdated data entry points.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing, subject to overriding legal framework and operational limitations.
  • File formal complaints with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) for any verified privacy concerns.

Acknowledgment & Consent

By registering under the eCARE EMR and PhilHealth YAKAP System, users acknowledge that they have read and understood this Data Privacy Statement and consent to the lawful processing of their personal and health information for the specific purposes stated herein.
