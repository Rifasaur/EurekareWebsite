General Information

eCare EMR is a PhilHealth-accredited electronic medical records system with an integrated YAKAP benefits module. It streamlines patient records, billing, laboratory/radiology, pharmacy, and mobile health documentation into one secure, audit-ready platform.

The system is designed for both private hospitals, clinics, and laboratories, as well as public health units, municipal health offices, and provincial hospitals.
